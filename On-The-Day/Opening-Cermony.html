BREAK THE LOOP
The "Break The Loop" challenge focuses on identifying any everyday frustrations or problems you've been having—whether they are university-specific or general issues encountered in daily life. Use technology to design practical solutions that improve real aspects of life.
How many times have you encountered a problem and hoped for someone else to find an effective solution? This is an invitation to fix what bugs you every day.

FAQ

ANY QUESTIONS?

Can I use AI?

Yes! AI tools are welcome and encouraged. Use them to boost your productivity — just make sure you understand what you're building and can explain it during judging.

Do I need coding experience?

Not at all! No-code tools, drag-and-drop builders, and AI assistants make it possible to build great projects without writing a single line of code.

How many members on the team?

Teams can have up to 4 members. Solo participation is also welcome — you can join a team during our team-building session at the start of the event!

Any other questions?

Come speak to one of the organisers — we're here to help! You can also ask in the Discord server for quick answers before and during the event.
