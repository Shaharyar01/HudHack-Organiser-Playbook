# 🎬 On The Day: Execution & Logistics

Running the day smoothly comes down to planning ahead and keeping people informed. Here is exactly how we set up the room, managed the volunteers, and ran the event on the day.

## 📋 Pre-Planning & Volunteer Roles
* **Plan the day beforehand:** Do not wing it. Create a clear master schedule for the organizing team.
* **Brief your volunteers:** Make sure every volunteer knows exactly what their job is before the doors open (e.g., who is working the check-in desk, who is managing the food delivery, and who is answering team questions).

## 🪑 Room Setup & Sponsor Tables
In a busy room, you need systems to track people and give sponsors a presence:
* **Table Numbers:** We put printed numbers on every single table (`HUDHACK 2026_ Table Numbers-2.pdf`). This made judging easy and helped us track where teams were sitting.
* **Discord QR Cards:** We placed Discord QR codes on every table (`HUDHACK 2026_ Discord QR Card-3.pdf`). This ensured everyone joined the server for announcements about food and delays.
* **Banners & Sponsors:** We hung up our physical HudHack banner and set up a dedicated table for Hackathons UK on the day so they could chat with students. 
* **Power Management:** Make sure you bring enough extension leads and tape down loose cables so nobody trips.

## 🖥️ Screens & Presentations
* **The Presentations:** We built our Opening and Closing slide decks using raw HTML and CSS (`Opening-Cermony.html` and `closing -cermony.html`). You don't have to do this next year; Canva or Google Slides works fine.
* **The Timetable:** When a presentation or workshop wasn't actively happening, we kept the day's timetable up on the main projector screens. This stops people from constantly asking when lunch is or when a talk starts.

## 🎮 Keeping the Energy Up
* **Mini-Games:** We ran mini-games throughout the day. These were really popular and gave people a much-needed break from staring at code. Definitely prepare a few quick games for next year.
* **The Participant Guide:** We gave students a physical guide (`HUDHACK 2026_ Participant Guide-7-2....pdf`) with the schedule and rules. We also pinned a PDF version in the Discord `#announcements` channel.
